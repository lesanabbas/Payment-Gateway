FEEDING INDIA

SMILING INDIA

Feeding India, a non-profit by Zomato is dedicated towards making India hunger-free. We provide over 150,000 nutritious meals to the underprivileged every day under the 'Daily Feeding Program'. The beneficiaries include - children, women, the elderly, specially-abled, and daily wage workers
